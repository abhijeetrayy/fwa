Section 1

General Questions

At FWA, we believe that transparency is the best tool for managing potential conflicts of interest. We maintain three core pillars:

  • A 100% Rebate Policy: We credit back all "retrocessions" or commissions received from Affiliated Alliances directly to the client's account. This ensures our financial interests are perfectly aligned with yours.
  • Logistical Advocacy: We maintain affiliations specifically to capture institutional-grade terms and discounted share classes that individual investors often cannot access directly.
  • Open Architecture: Every investment undergoes rigorous screening regardless of affiliations. We only recommend a product—affiliated or otherwise—if it is the best fit for your family's long-term objectives.

The difference lies in the depth of the relationship and the breadth of the service. While a bank or asset manager focuses on specific products, a Family Office like FWA acts as your strategic architect.

We provide holistic management across all your assets. Because we maintain strategic alliances with top-tier providers, we act as an informed bridge to institutional-grade opportunities that are often unavailable to individual bank clients.

Additionally, we are independent—we have no products to sell and no quotas to meet. Our only obligation is to you.

We operate a non-discretionary model because we believe clients should be active participants, always engaged and constantly learning. We want to help not only to manage your wealth, but to provide you with the knowledge you need to make critical decisions—now and in the future.

Our corporate services are focused on sophisticated, qualified clients who typically have investable assets of $5 million or more and seek institutional-quality family office services.

Section 3

Fees & Costs

Our compensation is solely derived from the families we serve through an established fee structure, maintaining transparency and simplicity.

We do not receive compensation from any managers, service providers, or financial institutions. When we receive commissions or "retrocessions" from affiliated managers, we credit 100% of those amounts back to your account.

Family office fees vary based on the complexity of your situation and the scope of services required. We provide a customized fee proposal after our initial discovery process.

While we don't disclose specific fee schedules publicly, we are committed to full transparency. You'll always know exactly what you're paying and what you're receiving in return.

Many families find that the value we provide—through cost savings, tax optimization, and better investment outcomes—exceeds our fees significantly.

We do not charge performance-based fees. Our fee structure is designed to align our interests with yours without creating incentives for excessive risk-taking.

We believe this approach promotes better long-term decision-making for your family's wealth.

Section 4

Getting Started

The first step is a confidential introductory conversation. This is an opportunity for us to learn about your situation and for you to learn about our approach.

There's no obligation—we want to ensure there's a good fit before we proceed. You can schedule a consultation through our contact page or by calling us directly.

For our initial conversation, you don't need to prepare anything specific. We'll ask questions about your current situation, your goals, and your concerns.

If we decide to proceed to a formal engagement, we'll provide a detailed checklist of documents and information we'll need to conduct our comprehensive assessment.

The onboarding timeline varies based on the complexity of your situation, but typically takes 4-8 weeks from our initial meeting to full implementation.

This includes discovery meetings, comprehensive assessment, strategy development, and initial implementation. We believe in being thorough rather than rushing—your wealth was built over a lifetime, and it deserves careful attention.
