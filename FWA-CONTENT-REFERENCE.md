## FAQ - SECTION 1: GENERAL QUESTIONS

FAQ_General_Q1: "How does FWA manage conflicts of interest with affiliated financial institutions?"
FAQ_General_A1: "At FWA, we believe that transparency is the best tool for managing potential conflicts of interest. We maintain three core pillars: (1) A 100% Rebate Policy - we credit back all 'retrocessions' or commissions received from Affiliated Alliances directly to the client's account. (2) Logistical Advocacy - we maintain affiliations to capture institutional-grade terms and discounted share classes. (3) Open Architecture - every investment undergoes rigorous screening regardless of affiliations."

FAQ_General_Q2: "What is the difference between FWA and a bank's private banking unit?"
FAQ_General_A2: "The difference lies in the depth of the relationship and the breadth of the service. While a bank or asset manager focuses on specific products, a Family Office like FWA acts as your strategic architect. We provide holistic management across all your assets. Because we maintain strategic alliances with top-tier providers, we act as an informed bridge to institutional-grade opportunities that are often unavailable to individual bank clients."

FAQ_General_Q3: "What is discretionary management? Does FWA offer this service?"
FAQ_General_A3: "Discretionary management typically means that your advisor is authorized to act on your behalf regarding the execution of your wealth management mandate without receiving your explicit permission. We operate a non-discretionary model because we want clients to be active participants, always engaged and constantly learning. We help not only to manage their wealth, but to provide them with the knowledge they need to make critical decisions – now and in the future."

FAQ_General_Q4: "Will I lose control of my assets if I work with FWA?"
FAQ_General_A4: "No, you will not. While we insist on keeping your family involved in critical decision-making, we also handle administrative tasks and support you in implementing your specific wealth management mandate."

## FAQ - SECTION 2: WORKING WITH FWA

FAQ_Working_Q1: "Does FWA provide education and training?"
FAQ_Working_A1: "Yes. We work with you and your family to create custom learning experiences including family meetings to focus on: Family decision protocol, Succession planning, Wealth transfer, Family governance structures, Leadership roles and structures, and Next generation education."

FAQ_Working_Q2: "Can FWA help reduce family conflict?"
FAQ_Working_A2: "Communication strategy is an important part of any wealth enterprise. While we cannot promise family harmony, we can promise to work with each family member to make sure they can voice concerns, express interests, discuss challenges and participate in planning for the future. This facilitated communication often leads to reduced conflict because family members feel their voices have been heard."

FAQ_Working_Q3: "Do I need to leave my current banking relationships?"
FAQ_Working_A3: "There is no need for you to change banks. As your advocate, we work with each of your service providers to ensure they are working toward your best interests. Through consolidated reporting, we review transactions, confirm pricing, review commissions, identify errors and see that what you agreed to pay is what you are being charged."

FAQ_Working_Q4: "Does FWA create holistic financial plans?"
FAQ_Working_A4: "Yes, we do. We create holistic financial plans to encompass all areas of your wealth enterprise, including wealth planning, investments, family governance, data aggregation, tax planning and more – the plans we create for clients are customized to address their needs, concerns and challenges."
