About Us

Stewards of Your Family Legacy

Wealth brings freedom, but it also brings complexity. Since 2009, Family Wealth Advisors (FWA) has provided the strategic clarity, architectural planning, and peace of mind that allow families to flourish across generations.

Our Story

Professional Management for Your Personal Wealth

Family Wealth Advisors was established in Herzliya in 2009 by Daniel Peretz, a financial markets veteran with over three decades of global experience.

After years in the institutional world, Daniel recognized a critical flaw in how wealthy families were being served. While they had access to excellent bankers, lawyers, accountants and asset managers, these professionals worked in silos. No one was looking at the "Big Picture." The banker didn't know about the estate plan, and the accountant wasn't coordinating with the investment manager.

FWA was built to close that gap.

We were founded on a simple premise: A family enterprise requires a CFO, just like a business enterprise. We set out to create a firm that would sit on the same side of the table as the family - selling no products, accepting no hidden commissions, and offering purely objective orchestration of the family's financial life.

What We Do

From Complexity to Clarity

We do not just manage portfolios; we manage the entire family enterprise.

Our role is to act as the central architect of your wealth. We coordinate every moving part of your financial life - investments, taxation, legal structures, and family governance - into a single, unified strategy.

Our mission is simple: to remove the burden of financial management from your shoulders, allowing you to focus on your business, your passions, and your family, confident that your financial house is in perfect order.

Our Standard

Uncompromised Objectivity

Fee-Only Model

Our only compensation comes from you. We never receive commissions from third parties.

Total Alignment

If we do receive a "retrocession" or commission from a fund, we credit 100% of it back to your account.

Fiduciary Mindset

We are not salespeople. We are fiduciaries, bound ethically and professionally to act solely in your best interest.
