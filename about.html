About Us

A Legacy of Trust & Generational Thinking

Since 2009, we've been the trusted stewards of family wealth—providing the strategic clarity and personal attention that every family deserves.

Our Story

Founded on a Simple Belief

Family Wealth Advisors was established in Herzliya in 2009 by Daniel Peretz, a financial markets veteran with over 30 years of global experience. The founding premise was straightforward: affluent families deserve the same quality of financial guidance and coordination that was once available only to institutions and ultra-high-net-worth individuals.

We recognized that successful families often struggle not from a lack of resources, but from a lack of integrated oversight. Investments are siloed. Tax advisors don't communicate with estate planners. The big picture gets lost.

Our solution was to create a true "Personal CFO" service—a single point of coordination for all financial matters, driven by your goals and governed by your values.

What Sets Us Apart

The qualities that make our approach genuinely different from traditional advisors.

Fee-Only Independence

We never receive commissions from product sales. Our only revenue comes directly from clients, ensuring our advice is always aligned with your best interests—not a product sponsor's.
